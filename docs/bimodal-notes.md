# BiModal Notes From Shared Material

## Working With Us

- Global IT consulting firm focused on DevOps, DevSecOps, and development.
- They state that about 85% of projects are DevOps.
- They emphasize Kubernetes and Terraform, especially scaling and security.
- Their preferred model is codified change: Terraform changes, peer review,
  testing, CD deployment, and validation through monitoring.
- They value real-time collaboration in US Pacific working hours.
- Full-time engineers are expected to lead other elastic engineers and manage
  client delivery.
- Their PreHire material emphasizes project management, timesheets, remote
  teamwork, Agile methods, and recurring demos.

## Interview FAQ

- Panel interview.
- They care about leadership, reasoning, observability of past environments,
  and why decisions were made.
- Screen sharing is heavily weighted.
- NDA-sensitive work should be sanitized. Their document specifically mentions
  removing credentials and hostnames, and avoiding secret-sauce algorithms.
- Recording can be turned off if explicitly requested.

## Implication

The strongest demo is not a private repo tour. It is a prepared, sanitized
artifact that proves the same skills while modeling client confidentiality.
